About BDJM

"Rooted in Tradition, Ready for the Future."

Our Heritage

A Journey of Excellence

Founded in the year 2000 with a deep commitment to serving the community of Ramnagar, BDJM Public School has grown into a premier educational hub in Nainital.

We believe that premium education should never be out of reach. Over the years, we have continually adapted to modern teaching methodologies while keeping our fee structure incredibly affordable, ensuring every child has access to multi-exposure growth and robust UK Board academics.

Our Vision

To create a transformative educational environment that provides high-quality, holistic education at a highly affordable fee structure, ensuring that brilliance is accessible to every student in our community.

Our Mission

To provide a rigorous, tech-enabled curriculum alongside holistic extracurricular activities, keeping costs low and comfort high with our expansive, safe, and affordable school transportation network.
