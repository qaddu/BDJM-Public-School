Legacy of Knowledge

Established in the year 2000, BDJM Public School has been a cornerstone of quality education in Ramnagar for over two decades. Our core values are deeply rooted in discipline, empathy, and academic rigor, ensuring every student develops into a well-rounded global citizen.

We believe that premium education should be accessible to all. We provide a holistic environment from Nursery through Class 10, ensuring that brilliant minds are never limited by cost.

Our Vision

"To provide quality education at a very affordable price, ensuring every child in Ramnagar has the opportunity to achieve academic and personal excellence."

Initiatives & Opportunities

Our School Specialties

We are committed to empowering our community through specialized government initiatives and robust sports programs.

25% Reserved Seats (RTE)

We provide 25% seat reservation for children from the Economically Weaker Section (EWS) under the Right to Education Act.

Govt. Scholarship Facility

State government scholarships are available for students from SC and OBC categories for Classes 1 through 10.

Affordable Transport

Safe and secure transportation is provided from Ramnagar to Mohan for a nominal fee of only ₹350.

Mukhyamantri Yojana

Our students are selected annually for the Mukhyamantri Khiladi Yojana, nurturing young athletic talent.

District Level Sports

Our students regularly participate in the Khel Mahakumbh, showcasing their skills and competing at the District Level.
